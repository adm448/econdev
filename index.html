A1 Class Project - Econ Development

Bridging the Digital Divide

Corinna Fehst, Adam Murray, Megan White

In this project we'll be looking at basic indicators of internet connectivity to see how they correlate to economic growth. We believe that investments in telecommunciations infrastructure will have a positive impact on growth broadly, and innovation specifically.

  • Internet access and innovation (Adam)
  • Internet access by gender (Corinna)
  • Internet access, economic growth, and labor force participation (Megan)
